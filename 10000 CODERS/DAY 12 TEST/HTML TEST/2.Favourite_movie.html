Super 30

Hrithik Roshan as mathematics teacher and educator Anand Kumar, who develops an educational program of the same title. The film is based on the life of Patna-based mathematician Anand Kumar, who runs the famed Super 30 program for IIT aspirants in Patna. Anand Kumar, a Mathematics genius from a modest family in Bihar, is on a mission to prove that even the poor can create some of the world's most genius minds
