A tea and snack shop in Hyderabad that is known for its service and quality products.
Tea is an aromatic beverage made from the leaves of the Camellia sinensis shrub, which is native to East Asia. Here are some details about tea: Origin Tea likely originated in the borderlands of southwestern China and northern Myanmar. Preparation Tea is made by pouring hot or boiling water over the leaves of the Camellia sinensis shrub. Types There are many different types of tea, including black, green, and post-fermented teas. Flavor Tea can have a variety of flavors, including cooling, bitter, astringent, sweet, nutty, floral, or grassy. Quality High-quality teas have complex flavor profiles with natural nuances that linger long after swallowing.
